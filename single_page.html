TetroONE

TetroONE is an AI-powered, cloud-based ERP platform designed to bring all your business operations into one intelligent system. It helps organizations manage finance, HR, supply chain, inventory, and operations with greater speed and accuracy.

Built for growing enterprises and large-scale organizations, TetroONE delivers real-time insights, automated workflows, and data-driven decision-making. From finance and procurement to HR and analytics, everything works together in one unified ERP ecosystem.

  • Real-time business insights
  • Intelligent process automation
  • Secure & Scalable cloud platform
  • Advanced reporting & analytics

TetroPAY

From innovative recruitment to automating payroll for any employee type and managing employee performance, attendance, and compliances — TetroPAY seamlessly integrates with Tetrosoft’s ecosystem, and bringing everything into one intelligent and scalable system.

  • Smart Recruitment Tool
  • Real-Time attendance
  • Leave & Shift Management
  • Automated payroll

TetroPOS

TetroPOS is a cloud-based, retail ERP system built to help your business run and grow efficiently. It streamlines billing, inventory, and compliance, making operations smoother than ever.

Designed for Large and small enterprises like giant manufacturing unit to Retail stores, TetroPOS ensures real-time inventory tracking, automated invoicing (including E-Invoice & E-Way Bill APIs), intuitive CRM and powerful sales analytics—all in one intuitive platform.

  • Real Time Inventory
  • Automated Invoicing & Billing
  • Fast, Secure Transactions
  • Sales Reporting & Analytics

Assetra

Assetra is an advanced asset management System that simplifies the way organizations track, manage, and optimize their assets. From procurement to auditing, Assetra provides end-to-end visibility, reduces losses, and ensures higher returns on every asset investment.

With powerful modules like contact & vendor management, real-time asset tracking, QR-based mapping, transfers, auditing, purchase integration, and finance tracking, Assetra centralizes all operations in one secure platform — making your asset management seamless, efficient, and completely transparent.

TexsuitePro

TexSuitePro is your all-in-one software for the garments and textile industry. From inventory and production to quality control and delivery, it streamlines every step of your operations. With intuitive tools, real-time insights, and automation, TexSuitePro helps you boost efficiency, reduce errors, and stay ahead in a competitive market.

With TexSuitePro, you can easily manage multiple production lines, track raw materials, and monitor order progress in real-time. Its customizable dashboards and detailed analytics empower managers to make smart decisions, optimize workflows, and ensure timely delivery your business grow efficiently and profitably.
